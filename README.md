### Data Source and Overview

This report analyze the financial performance of Simbi Logistics. The dataset used for this analysis was provided by the CEO of Simbi Logistics and it covers the period from 13th January 2025 to 31st March 2026. It consists of 442 rows and 12 columns. 


### Objectives

- Monitor profitability and provide clear visibility into daily, weekly, and monthly financial performance.
- Analyze order trends and patterns to understand business demand, operational performance, and revenue generation across different time periods.

 
### Tools

- ChatGPT (for Conversion)
- Excel (for Data Cleaning)
- Power BI (for Transformation and Analysis)


### Data Cleaning

1. PDF to Excel Conversion
2. Column Formatting
3. Editing and Proper Casing
4. Handling missing values
5. Removing duplicates

### Recommendations

- Monitoring Performance Regularly
  - The company should continue the use of Power BI dashboards to track profitability, expenses, and order trends for better decision-making.
- Controlling Major Expenses e.g. Fueling
  - The company should pack orders going in the same direction together, to improve delivery efficiency and reduce transportation cost. 
- Taking Advantage of the Peak Days and Special Days
  - The company should run promotions and ensure adequate rider availability on Thursdays, which have the highest customer demand. Also, ensure the availability of the riders on the special days like Valentine, Christmas etc…
- Boosting Orders During Slow Periods
  - The company should introduce discounts, loyalty rewards, or special offers on slower days, especially Sundays.
- Growing the Customer Base
  - The company should partner with more businesses, restaurants, supermarkets, and online vendors to increase order volume.
